CSIT Hackathon 2025

Date: July 25–26, 2025

Time: 10:00 AM – 10:00 AM (24-hour Hackathon)

Venue: Computer Lab, Padma Kanya Multiple Campus

Join us for an electrifying 24-hour coding marathon where creativity meets innovation! The CSIT Hackathon 2025 invites developers, designers, and idea generators to solve real-world problems using tech. Compete in teams, pitch your ideas, and win exciting prizes!

Highlights:

  • Team-based problem solving (2–4 members)
  • Workshops and mentoring during the event
  • Certificates for all participants
  • Attractive cash prizes and goodies
