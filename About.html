About PKCSIT Events Hub

Empowering students through organized events, workshops, and collaborative learning in the field of Computer Science and IT.

Who We Are

CSIT Events Hub is a student-centered initiative dedicated to organizing, promoting, and managing academic and extracurricular events within the CSIT department. Our mission is to provide students with a platform to engage in hands-on learning experiences beyond the classroom.

Our Goals

Our goals include organizing seminars, coding competitions, workshops, tech meetups, and exhibitions to enhance students' practical knowledge and professional growth. We aim to foster a vibrant and inclusive tech community within Padma Kanya Multiple Campus and beyond.

About the CSIT Department

The BSc CSIT program at Padma Kanya Multiple Campus is designed to provide students with a strong foundation in both computer science and information technology. It prepares students to meet the challenges of the rapidly evolving tech industry through academic excellence and project-based learning.
